# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This document outlines the requirements for creating an elite designer's portfolio website for Ediodiong, featuring a modern design with gold and black color scheme, grid layouts, and smooth scrolling interactions.

## Glossary

- **Portfolio_System**: The complete web application showcasing Ediodiong's design work
- **Hero_Section**: The main landing area with grid background and title
- **About_Section**: The section describing Ediodiong's specializations
- **Project_Gallery**: The grid-based image showcase with horizontal scrolling
- **Contact_Form**: The user interaction form for inquiries
- **Social_Links**: Navigation elements to social media profiles

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a potential client, I want to see an impressive hero section when I visit the portfolio, so that I immediately understand this is a professional designer's website.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL display a grid background pattern in the hero section
2. THE Portfolio_System SHALL render "HI, IM EDIODIONG" as the main title with "EDIODIONG" in gold color
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL display "brief introduction" text in white color below the title
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL show introduction text in grey color
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL use black as the primary background color

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to learn about Ediodiong's specializations, so that I can understand what services are offered.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL provide an about section describing specializations
2. THE Portfolio_System SHALL specify that Ediodiong specializes in logos and fliers
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL maintain the gold and black color scheme in the about section

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a potential client, I want to view Ediodiong's work in an engaging gallery format, so that I can assess the quality and style of the designs.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL display projects in a 3-column grid system
2. THE Portfolio_System SHALL populate each column with multiple images
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL make column 2 lead horizontally ahead of the other columns
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL implement horizontal overflow scrolling for the gallery
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL ensure all columns scroll at the same speed

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to see a diverse collection of portfolio items, so that I can fully appreciate the range and quality of Ediodiong's design work.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL provide at least 20 mock portfolio items for demonstration purposes
2. THE Portfolio_System SHALL include a balanced mix of logo and flier designs
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL display varied project titles and descriptions for each portfolio item
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL use placeholder images with different dimensions to simulate real portfolio variety
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL categorize all portfolio items appropriately as logos or fliers

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a potential client, I want to contact Ediodiong easily, so that I can inquire about design services.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL provide a contact form for user inquiries
2. THE Portfolio_System SHALL include social media links for additional contact options
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL maintain consistent styling with the overall design theme
